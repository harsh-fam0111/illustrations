## Illustrations

When I ask you to create illustrations, article images, body images, shot lists,
or anything in the "Xiaohei" hand-drawn style, use the `illustrations` skill
(.claude/skills/illustrations/SKILL.md). Follow its workflow: digest the source,
produce a shot list, then generate one image per shot. Don't invent your own
style — defer to the skill's Style DNA and QA checklist.
